2. Device Overview
Product Specifications
Product Name: PaceWell Internal Pacemaker
Classification: Class III Medical Device (FDA)
Intended Use: Provides electrical stimulation to regulate heartbeats in patients with arrhythmia.

Technical Specifications
Pulse Generator: Programmable output, 0.1–10V amplitude
Pacing Modes: Single-chamber, dual-chamber, and biventricular pacing
Battery Life: 8–12 years (Lithium-Iodine)
Lead System: Unipolar and bipolar electrode configurations
Frequency Range: 30–180 bpm, adjustable in 1 bpm increments
Power Supply: Hermetically sealed lithium battery
Operating Temperature: -10°C to 55°C

Safety Features
Automatic Shutdown: Protection against voltage spikes and overheating.
Current Limiter: Prevents surges beyond 10V.
Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) Shielding: Protects against external signal disruptions.

Connectivity
Programming Interface: Wireless telemetry for remote adjustments
Charging & Firmware Updates: Non-invasive inductive charging and firmware upgrades

5. Manufacturing Process Workflow
Step 1: Component Sourcing & Inspection
- Visual and functional testing against ISO and FDA standards.

Step 2: PCB Assembly
- SMT pick-and-place process → Reflow soldering → Automated Optical Inspection (AOI).

Step 3: Housing Production
- CNC machining of titanium casings.
- Surface finishing & quality control checks.

Step 4: Device Assembly
- PCB, battery, and components integrated.
- Laser welding ensures a hermetic seal.

Step 5: Calibration & Testing
- Pulse waveform accuracy validation.
- Voltage and current calibration.

Step 6: Sterilization
- Ethylene Oxide (EtO) sterilization.
- Residual gas verification for safety compliance.

Step 7: Packaging & Labeling
- FDA-compliant labeling with UDI codes.
- User manuals included in multi-language formats.
