**Project Overview:**  
We are seeking a manufacturing partner to produce a Left Ventricular Assist Device (LVAD) in compliance with international medical device standards. Below are the technical specifications and component requirements for the proposed LVAD system.

**Technical Specifications:**  
- **Device Type:** Implantable Mechanical Circulatory Support System  
- **Intended Use:** Provides hemodynamic support for patients with advanced heart failure  
- **Pump Type:** Axial flow or centrifugal flow blood pump  
- **Flow Rate:** Adjustable, 2–10 L/min  
- **Power Source:** Rechargeable lithium-ion battery, minimum 6-hour runtime  
- **Weight:** ≤ 400 grams (implantable unit)  
- **Noise Level:** ≤ 25 dB during operation  
- **Materials:** Biocompatible titanium and medical-grade polymers  
- **Coating:** Hemocompatible coating to prevent thrombosis  
- **Sensors:** Integrated flow and pressure sensors for real-time monitoring  
- **Connectivity:** Wireless telemetry for remote monitoring and programming  
- **Backup System:** Redundant power supply with automatic switching  
- **Sterilization:** Must withstand EtO or gamma sterilization  

**Component Requirements:**  

**1. Core Electronic Components**  
| Component | Specification | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Microcontroller Unit (MCU) | ARM Cortex-M4 or equivalent | Controls pump speed and monitoring functions |
| Battery | Lithium-ion, 14V, 5000mAh | Rechargeable, with fail-safe protection |
| Communication Module | BLE 5.0 / RF Telemetry | Enables wireless communication |
| Power Management IC | Medically certified PMIC | Ensures efficient power usage and safety |
| Sensors | High-precision pressure and flow sensors | Real-time hemodynamic monitoring |

**2. Mechanical Components**  
| Component | Specification | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Pump Housing | Titanium Grade 23 | Biocompatible, corrosion-resistant |
| Impeller | Polyetheretherketone (PEEK) | Low-friction, blood-contact safe |
| Drive Mechanism | Magnetic levitation / Hydrodynamic bearings | Reduces wear and thrombosis risk |
| Tubing | Medical-grade silicone | Flexible, kink-resistant |

**3. Control and Monitoring Unit (External Controller)**  
- **Display:** OLED touchscreen for patient and clinician interface  
- **Alarm System:** Audio and visual alerts for low battery, occlusion, or malfunction  
- **Data Storage:** Minimum 30-day operational history storage  
- **Connectivity:** USB-C and Bluetooth for data transfer  
- **Battery Backup:** Minimum 12-hour runtime in case of primary failure  

**Regulatory and Compliance Requirements:**  
- Must comply with **ISO 13485:2016** (Medical Device Quality Management System)  
- Electrical safety and EMC compliance as per **IEC 60601-1 & IEC 60601-1-2**  
- Must meet **ISO 14708-5** for Active Implantable Medical Devices  
- Biocompatibility per **ISO 10993-1**  
- U.S. FDA Class III regulatory approval required
